Café Story

Our cafe, located in the middle of the city, opened in 2024 with the goal of creating a welcoming space for friends and family to share laughter, tales, and a passion for coffee. Our journey began with a strong desire for excellence, sustainability, and community.We acquire our beans from local growers to assure the freshest flavors while also supporting the local economy.Our professional baristas take great care in making each cup, from rich and silky lattes to bold and adventurous cold brews. Whether you are a coffee connoisseur or simply seeking for a warm and pleasant place to unwind, we invite you to visit our café and enjoy its warmth. See you there, coffee lover!


Meet the Team

Brian Keinth Arnejo

Brian Keinth Arnejo is a 21-year-old aspiring computer scientist student at Palawan State University's main campus. Being a computer science student led him to become a coffee shop owner. A coffee shop where everyone can tell their story. and make it truly inspiring for others, so that they are encouraged to share their own experiences inspired by the comfortable cup cafe.

Aneza Jamion

Aneza, a 20-year-old aspiring computer scientist, is currently in her third year at PalSu's main campus. She used to dislike coffee because of environmental effects and the dread of palpitations. But as time passed, she realized that once in a while, we all need a coffee.A coffee that improves energy, a coffee that pushes you to accomplish things correctly at a time with productivity, and a coffee that brings everyone together everyone together. So she opened a coffee shop that provides a wide choice of coffee, blended, and non-coffee drinks
